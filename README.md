# Krewe-House
Krewe House is a new and modern way to celebrate New Orleans' infamous Mardi Gras parades. My amazing business idea entails an indoor Mardi Gras Parade experience, complete with parking, bathrooms, dining, live music and performances.  The consumers will pay to view the parades from the inside of Krewe House, a sleek, converted warehouse located Uptown near the St. Charles streetcar line. Patrons will have the choice of sitting or standing with their unimpeded view of the parades, floats, performers, and marching bands. For a more upscale and VIP experience, you can even purchase tickets to dine while watching the parades.
